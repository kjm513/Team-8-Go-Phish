Go Phish is a website all about efficiency.
Are you a curious Internet dweller? Perhaps an overtaxed Security Analyst trying to solve a ticket that requires a lot of Open Source Intelligence? Or maybe, you just really like fish and ended up here by accident.
Whatever the case, listed below is the core functionality of this site and what it should be used for.

  • This site is an aggregator of OSINT information.
  • URLs may be input into the text box on the home page. You must click submit afterwards.
  • APIs are called and referenced for the collection of information on the submitted URL.
  • The following OSINT APIs will be used: VirusTotal and URLScan
  • Results will be returned in a new tab to keep you organized and clutter free!
